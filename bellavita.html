FAQs

Does perfume age your skin?

No, perfume doesn’t age your skin. However, the chances of premature ageing increase if an expired perfume is applied.


Does perfume last longer on clothes or the skin?

If you want your perfume to work the best, then avoid applying it to clothes. Fragrances work the best when applied right to the skin.


How many sprays of perfume should I wear?

To identify the number of sprays, check the perfume concentration behind the bottle. You can easily apply 3-4 sprays if your perfume is lightweight and refreshing. However, 1-2 sprays are sufficient for intensive or heavy fragrances.


What is cologne vs perfume?

The primary difference between cologne and perfume is that cologne consists of a lower concentration of fragrance oils than perfumes. Cologne comes with a lighter and more subtle scent. However, one can identify a more potent scent with perfume.


Why do girls put perfume on their ankles?

Spraying perfume on your ankles makes it easier to spread the scent wherever you go. The reason behind this is that most of the time, we are in motion, and it helps spread fragrance around quickly.
